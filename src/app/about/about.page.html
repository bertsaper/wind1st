About Wind 1st

For bicycle commuters, wind direction and velocity are important pieces of information. Finding these on most weather apps takes a lot of digging.

This app is designed to make wind direction and velocity foremost.

The app is built with the OpenWeather API and the Google Places API.

FAQ

Why is the location entered in Settings different than the location displayed on Weather?

Your longitude and latitude are returned from Google Places and sent to OpenWeather. OpenWeather often uses a different name for the same locale.
